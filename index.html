Paula Lago

Assistant Professor at Concordia University in Montreal, QC, CA.
Research in Ambient Intelligence, Activity Recognition, and Technology for Health-care

I study how pervasive computing can improve people’s quality of life by adapting to their personal routines and current activity.

  • Work with me

My Research

Sensors and Data Streams

Using sensors embedded in objects of smart environments or sensors embedded in wearable devices to understand people’s needs or to enable personal awareness and reflection of behavior.

Pattern mining and machine learning

Making sense of sensor data by recognizing activities, learning frequent patterns and understanding routines

Healthcare applications

Sensing and analysis brought together for:

  • Health monitoring at home
  • Automatic nursing record creation>
